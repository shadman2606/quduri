In the Name of Allah, the Inspirer of Truth.

Welcome to Year 4 of the ʿĀlim program, and specifically Mukhtaṣar al-Qudūrī class!

You will be doing two sections of Qudūrī with two different teachers in shā Allah. I will be teaching the first section, which starts from Kitāb al-Buyūʿ. The first day of class will be Tuesday, September 3.

For the first day, please prepare 5 pages from the beginning of Kitāb al-Buyūʿ. If you do not have the book yet, you can prepare using this online pdf. Note, this online version is a bit old and might not have the same size pages as the physical book you will receive. It's not a big deal for the first day. From the second class onward, we will count pages based on the physical book.
